Giving Gifts

Seth Godin: "I've found that giving gifts is transformative. It makes me better. It clarifies my thinking and allows me to do better work. I see things differently when I'm focused on opening doors for other people, and more often than not, my doors are opened as well. At the same time, I sleep better... I feel like I'm treating people as I'd like to be treated."



Seth is a fantastic Author. He often writes about giving gifts; an unconventional thought process regarding everything we do, particularly in our jobs. I love this concept, that we should perform our daily lives as though each interaction, each task, is a gift to be given. It's not about everything being done free of charge, it's simply a way to approach what we do knowing that a gift blesses those who recieve it as much as it blesses those who give it.
